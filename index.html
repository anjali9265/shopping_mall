About



GALAXY mall is one of the largest mall in Kerala.It is one of the most visited places in Kerala.Spanning 12 acres with a total built up area of 2 million square
feet, the mall has a total retail space of 1.3 million square feet.The mall was opened on may 2014.It contains more than 200 outlets which includes food
courts, restaurants, family entertainment zones, a multiplex.The estimated cost for this project is more than 15 million.The property owned and managed by
Galaxy group,which has extensive business in retail,hospitality,real estate etc.

Galaxy mall in kochi was their maiden retail venture in india.The group’s second and third giant malls are under construction in kozhikode,Kerala and
lucknow, Uttar pradesh.Galaxy mall with an impressive architecture is an eclectic ensemble of retail offerings spread across four storeys .It comes with
everything that is required to whet the desire of a discerning shopper, from branded apparels, fashion accessories, jewellery, gifts and electronic items to
food court, restaurants, coffee shops and excellent entertainment and fun options.Galaxy mall is kerala’s premier shopping destination .
